Privacy Policy

Daily — Minimalist Habit Tracker  ·  Last updated 31 May 2026

The short version

No data is collected, stored on any server, or transmitted anywhere.
All habit data lives only on your device, in your browser's local storage.
No account required. No sign-up. No analytics. No ads. Ever.

01

What data we collect

Daily does not collect any personal data. We have no servers, no databases, and no infrastructure to receive or store user information.

The extension stores your habits, daily completions, streak data, and display preferences exclusively on your own device using the browser's built-in chrome.storage.local API. This data is private to your browser and is never transmitted anywhere.

02

Local storage

The extension uses chrome.storage.local to persist your habit list, completion history, streak counts, theme preferences, tracker duration, and display mode settings.

This data is stored in Chrome's local extension storage on your device. It is not synced to any external service. It is not shared with any third party. Deleting the extension removes all locally stored data.

03

Third-party services

The extension loads typography from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This is a standard web font request used solely for display purposes. No personal data is included in this request beyond the standard network information sent with any HTTP request (such as your IP address), which is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

No other third-party services, SDKs, analytics platforms, or advertising networks are used.

04

Permissions

Daily requests one browser permission: storage. This is used solely to save your habits and settings locally on your device as described above. No other permissions are requested or used.

05

Children's privacy

Daily does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children. The extension is a local-only productivity tool with no data collection of any kind.

06

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date. Since the extension collects no data, material changes to this policy are unlikely.
