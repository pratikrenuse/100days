The person

The person behind the work

I thrive in the zero-to-one phase: no playbook, no team, no clarity. The job is to find the growth loop and run it until it works. Building something from nothing is the most fulfilling work I know.

Today my attention is on AI: where it changes how marketers operate and where the next generation of products gets built. The operator instincts from consumer hardware and fintech apply here, only faster.

Outside the work, a long practice of stillness and travel across many countries taught me to listen before I plan, and to back the people I work with as much as the work itself.
