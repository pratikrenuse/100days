The person

The person behind the work

Over the last decade I have built startups from zero, taking Infento from zero to eight-figure revenue, raising $848K on Kickstarter for the #1 STEM project in the US, and taking VU Switzerland to Olympic-level recognition. I have mentored 100+ founders through Techstars, EBRD, Seedstars, and the UN; coached graduates at Harvard, LSE, HEC Paris, and Oxford; and spoken at conferences across four continents including three TEDx stages.

Beyond the work, I am a Vipassana practitioner, a solo traveler across 20+ countries, and the creator of Vinnyana Visuals, a community of 10,000+ people who share the joy of turning complex ideas into simple illustrations.
