The person

The person behind the work

I work best in the zero-to-one phase, where there's no playbook, no team, no clarity. The job is to find the loop that grows the company and run it until it does. I have done this multiple times across consumer hardware, SaaS, marketplaces, and fintech.

My approach is shaped by two things outside the work. Vipassana meditation, which I have practiced and served at silent retreats for years, taught me that most decisions stall because of emotion, not information. And travel across 20+ countries taught me that what works in one market rarely transfers cleanly to the next, so I start every engagement by listening before I plan.

I am happy to be reached for serious consulting, advisory, board, and angel work. I respond to every message personally.

"Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication."
